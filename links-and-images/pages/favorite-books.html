In this page you will find an image and some info about my favorite books!

The Plague - Camus

Written by Albert Camus, this book is about a city where a dangerous disease spreads.

People become scared and isolated, but some of them try to help others. The story shows how people act in difficult situations and teaches ideas like helping each other, staying strong, and finding meaning in life.
Listen, Little Man! Listen, Little Man!

Written by Wilhelm Reich, this book talks about everyday people and their behavior. It explains that many people follow others without thinking and are often afraid to speak up or take responsibility.

The main message of the book is that people should think for themselves, be brave, and take control of their own lives instead of blindly following others.
